About

I'm a combined B.S/M.S student in the Department of Computer Science, Johns Hopkins University. I'm interested in doing research in problems of different categories in the field of natural language processing (both text and speech).

During my undergraduate years, I have also spent a few memorable summers either interning at Markle Lab, Battle Lab, Yarowsky Lab in CLSP. I grew up in Delhi, India where my profile photo was also taken.

In my free time, you'll find me trying to learn new languages. I have training in over 5 languages, with many more to come!
