Milind Agarwal

Hello! I'm Milind Agarwal and I recently graduated with a combined B.S/M.S in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University. I do research in natural language processing, machine translation, and information visualization.

I will be starting my PhD in Computer Science at George Mason University this Fall as Prof. Antonios Anastasopoulos's advisee! I am interested in NLP problems that deal with limited data, since that's where I think it gets most interesting! I’m quite passionate about making useful language technologies for the many languages of India.

During my undergraduate years, I have also spent a few memorable summers interning with Prof. David Yarowsky , Prof. Janet Markle, and Prof. Alexis Battle. I grew up in Delhi, India where my profile photo was also taken at Cafe Di Milano.

I love to learn different languages, especially ones from South Asia, and find that I'm in flow when I do this. I am a native speaker of English and Hindi . I am quite comfortable in Urdu , Punjabi , and French . I'm also actively learning Telugu and Persian .
